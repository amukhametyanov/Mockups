The Demo Club Event starts Thursday, November 5th at NoonET.

A demo golf club has been previously enjoyed by another player(s). It may have been used outside, as a rental set, at a retailer as a test club in the simulator, to perform custom fittings, or at vendor sponsored events at driving ranges. The condition of these clubs can range from nearly new to normal signs of wear for a used-club (e.g. blemishes, ball-marks, minor scratches, worn grips, etc.). Demo clubs do not come with a headcover or wrench, however they are covered under a one (1) year vendor warranty.

Golf Town’s Demo Club Event features current models of “demo” golf clubs from the best brands in golf at up to 40% off the original MSRP.

[New for 2020] Demo clubs will NOT be available for sale in Golf Town stores for this year’s event. Out of an abundance of caution for our customers and staff, this event will be offered online only.

Demo clubs may be returned or exchanged at any Golf Town store or by mail to a Return Processing Centre (please see the back of your packing slip for more details) within 30 days from the date of purchase.

Note: Golf Town stores will not have any demo club inventory.

The inventory of demo clubs for this year’s event are in the best condition we have seen in recent years, however please remember that that most demo clubs will show visible, cosmetic signs of use including but not limited to blemishes ball marks and grip wear. All demo clubs sold in the Demo Event have been inspected by one of our golf club experts to ensure it performs like new.

All demo clubs offered in the Demo Event carry a one (1) year warranty from the date of purchase.

Each demo club has been cleaned and inspected by a Golf Town club expert before being shipped to certify that it will perform like new.

Most orders are processed within 2-4 business days, however shipping times will vary depending on the carrier and final destination. Once your order is picked up by UPS you will receive a shipment confirmation email with a UPS tracking number that you can use to conveniently track your order on UPS.com.

Headcovers and wrenches are not included with the purchase of demo golf clubs. However, generic headcovers are available to purchase on golftown.com should you need one. Most wrenches are now universal (with Srixon being the only exception) so as long as you have a wrench, it should work with your new demo club purchase.
