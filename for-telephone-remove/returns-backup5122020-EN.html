Store Returns, Warranty and Repairs

To help uphold the required health standards and to stop the spread of COVID-19, Sporting Life stores are closed until further notice. You will be able to return and pick up items once our stores re-open, when we can ensure a healthy and safe environment for all customers and staff.

Online Return Policy – Worry free returns!

 Online Store

Sporting Life offers a 60-day return policy for all online purchases. In order to qualify for a refund, please include your invoice in the parcel. All products must be unworn, unused, and in original condition. Returns will be processed back to the original method of payment.
  • If we receive your return after the 60-day time period, the return will be processed onto a Sporting Life gift card that will be emailed to the purchasing customer.
  • Online purchases may also be returned, within 60 days, or exchanged at any Sporting Life retail location along with a copy of the invoice.
  • Please note that, if you send your return back to the Sporting Life Online Store via Canada Post, we will only refund your return once the parcel has been processed at our Sporting Life online processing facility.
  • All returns are subject to a $10.00 + tax Restocking Fee. This is a fee and is charged per order, not for each item within an order. The $10.00 + tax fee is reflected in your return amount, for example a return for $100.00 would translate into a return value less the $10.00+tax.
  • Sporting Life does not offer returns or exchanges on Gift Cards or products that are worn or damaged.
  • All swimwear and undergarments are FINAL SALE.


 In-Store

Sporting Life offers a 60-day return policy. Returns must include a receipt and will be processed to the original method of payment. Returns after the 60-day period will be issued to a Sporting Life Gift card.
  • Sporting Life does not offer returns or exchanges on Gift Cards or products that are worn or damaged.
  • All swimwear and undergarments are FINAL SALE.

Warranty & Repairs:

If you have a question regarding warranty or repair for your product, please contact by email at csr@sportinglife.ca. Please do not attempt to contact the manufacturer directly.


Price Adjustment Policy:

Sporting Life will not honor price adjustments for:

  • Black Friday
  • Black Friday Week
  • Cyber Monday
  • Boxing Day Week
  • Any Flash Sale or Time sensitive promotions

Sporting Life will honor a one time price adjustment for orders placed online that are further marked down or go on sale. In order to qualify for a price adjustment you must contact our customer service department within a 14 day period of your order being placed and your size and color must be in available and in stock to qualify. If you would like a price adjustment, please contact Customer Service by email at customerservice@sportinglife.ca


Product and Price Discrepancies:

At Sporting Life, we carefully monitor product information, promotions and pricing to always ensure accuracy. Unfortunately, certain errors out of our control may occur from time to time. Please be advised that all prices are subject to change at any time and without any prior notice.

The items and prices available on the SportingLife.ca website, are not necessarily the same as the items and pricing within our "bricks and mortar" retail locations.


Online Exchange Policy:

If you would like to exchange a product you have received, please contact the Customer Service Department by email at customerservice@sportinglife.ca. All exchanges are pending stock availability of the item(s) you request and we cannot guarantee the item you request will be in stock.


Online Return Process:

To send your item(s) back to Sporting Life please contact our online customer service department by email customerservice@sportinglife.ca to obtain a free pre-paid return shipping label, please attach the prepaid Return to Sender label to the outside of the box. You can also drop your parcel off at your local Canada Post. Once we receive your parcel, we will process the return.

Original shipping fees will not be refunded.


Paypal - Returns and Refunds:

If you choose to return an item from an online order that was purchased using Paypal to a retail store, Sporting Life cannot refund the Paypal account.

For refunds back to your paypal account please return your order to the Sporting Life Ecommerce Online Store, please contact our customer service department by email customerservice@sportinglife.ca to obtain a free pre-paid Canada Post label. Sporting Life will then credit the paypal account used when processing your return. If you have any questions please contact customer service.
