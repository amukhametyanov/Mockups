 Holiday Return & Exchange Policy

Online Store

Sporting Life offers a 60-day return policy for all online purchases. In order to qualify for a refund, please include your invoice in the parcel. All products must be unworn, unused, and in original condition. Returns will be processed back to the original method of payment.

  • If we receive your return after the 60-day time period, the return will be processed onto a Sporting Life gift card that will be emailed to the purchasing customer.
  • Online purchases may also be returned, within 60 days, or exchanged at any Sporting Life retail location along with a copy of the invoice.
  • Please note that, if you send your return back to the Sporting Life Online Store via Canada Post, we will only refund your return once the parcel has been processed at our Sporting Life online processing facility.
  • Sporting Life does not offer returns or exchanges on swimwear, undergarments or products that are worn or damaged.
