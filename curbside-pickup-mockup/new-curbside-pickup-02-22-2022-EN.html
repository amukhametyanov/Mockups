How it works

1. Place Your Order Online

To view product availability at your preferred location, click “Find in Store” when placing your order. Select your location. Close the window and select “Add to Cart.” During checkout, select “In-Store Pickup” under the delivery options.

2. Wait for the “Ready for Pickup” Email

We’ll send you an email to let you know when your order is ready to be picked up. For your safety, please review the “Curbside Pickup Process” instructions and the order Shipment ID included in the email.

3. Call 15 Minutes Before Arrival

When you are about 15 minutes away, please call the location and provide our staff with a description of your vehicle. Upon your arrival, communicate your shipment ID to our staff and have a valid piece of government photo ID ready for order verification. Pop your trunk open ready for our team to place your order in your trunk.

Curbside Pickup

Once your order is ready, a “Ready for Pick Up” email will be sent to you. When you are about 15 minutes away, please alert our staff by calling the store. Our team will ensure your order is ready and waiting for you by the designated entrance. Upon your arrival, communicate your shipment ID to our staff and have a valid piece of government photo ID ready for order verification. Pop your trunk open ready and our team will place your order in your trunk.

If your Shipment ID does not match our records, your order will be returned to the store and we will investigate the order.

Once an order is placed it cannot be changed or cancelled. Please contact the store and reject the order.

Sporting Life offers a 60-day return period for all purchases made online. Shipping fee is non-refundable. Returns with a valid receipt can be made by mail or at any of our reopened locations. Refunds on eligible items will be issued to the original form(s) of payment. Please see below for instructions on how to make a return by mail.

We will only accept returns on items that are in its original condition: unworn, unused, and unwashed with all the original tags and packaging intact. We do not accept returns or exchanges on gift cards, swimwear, undergarments, or products that are worn or damaged.

If you prefer to return your item(s) to us when our locations reopen, please note that we will be extending the return period for an additional 30 days once our stores reopen.
