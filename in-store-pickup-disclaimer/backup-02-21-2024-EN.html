Curbside Pickup is available at participating locations.

Curbside pickup orders take approximately 2 hours to fulfill. Once your order has been processed you will receive an email and a text message confirming that your "Order is Ready for Pickup". You will also receive subsequent reminder emails and text messages in case your order is not picked up. At pickup, please have a valid piece of government issued photo ID ready for verification. Government issued photo ID must match the name and address on the order. You will have 7 days to pickup your order before it is cancelled.
