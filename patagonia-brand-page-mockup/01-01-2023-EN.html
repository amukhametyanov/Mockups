Know How Your Clothes are made

Everything Patagonia makes has an impact on people and the planet. That’s why they use recycled or organic materials when possible, support Fair Trade factories and guarantee, for life, everything they make.

Ironclad Guarantee

Patagonia products are built to endure, because they know prioritizing durability results in consuming less energy, wasting less water and creating less trash. If you are not satisfied with one of their products, return it to Patagonia for a repair, replacement or refund.

Set your sights on adventure with a wide selection of outdoor apparel and accessories from Patagonia. During snowy winter commutes through the city, wet spring hikes along the trail or sunny summer weekends by the water, make the most of every moment with performance gear that is built to last. Find all the essentials you need to excel through every season and stay protected from the elements. Combining the best of style and social responsibility, each item in the Patagonia collection is specially crafted with the planet in mind. The brand is moving towards a goal of 100% renewable and recycled raw materials to reduce harmful pollution from carbon emissions. Patagonia has also committed 1% of sales to the preservation and restoration of the environment for more than 35 years. Discover the latest arrivals and popular styles including cozy fleece sweaters, insulated jackets, raincoats, graphic tees, active pants, shorts, swimwear, bags, hats and weather-resistant outerwear for men, women and kids.
