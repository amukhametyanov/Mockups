Bike Home Delivery FAQs

Ordering Online

Orders for bikes will be processed on our website like any online order. Simply add your desired bike to your cart and proceed to checkout as normal.

Please note, that we can only accept orders for bikes within our delivery zone. During checkout, the option for “Sporting Life Bicycle Delivery” will appear under the “Select Shipping Method” section when a qualifying shipping address has been entered.

Our Customer Care team can look into any technical issues that may have occurred and help you place your order.

Our team of technicians will assemble all bikes to ensure quality and safety. Bikes will be disinfected by our team prior to delivery and during curbside pickup.

It can take 3 to 5 business days for your bike to be delivered.

When you place a new order, it will be submitted to our team the next business day. A member of our team will contact you by phone to confirm your order. Your bike will be built on the same or following day, time permitting, and then be placed in the delivery queue. The day after your bike has been built, a member of our team will contact you to arrange delivery.

On the day of your scheduled delivery, our driver will contact you to ensure delivery can be made and provide you with a window of time.

Yes, you can! We also invite you to take advantage of our 20% off Bike Accessories offer. To qualify, you must add the items to same order as your new bike. The discount is automatically applied at checkout. Some exceptions apply.

Our team can pre-install accessories onto your new bike before delivery if they are available at the same location as the bike. Items that do not require installation (helmets, shoes, gloves, apparel, etc.) or are sourced from another location will be delivered separately by Canada Post.

Please note that we cannot modify an order once it has been placed. If you would like to change your order in any way (add new items, change the mailing address, etc.), place a new one with your preferred items and/or the correct information and cancel your order with our Customer Care team right away.

We do have models that are not listed online and we may be able to place a special order for you. Please get in contact with a participating location for availability.

Our Customer Care team will be happy to assist you. You can reach them via email at customerservice@sportinglife.ca.

Contactless Delivery

Bike delivery is free!

Our delivery zone is determined by the location of our participating store. Click here for our current delivery zone.

The day after your bike has been built, a member of our team will contact you to arrange delivery. On the day of your scheduled delivery, our driver will safely contact you by phone while en route to ensure delivery can be made, provide you with a window of time, and arrange a process for contactless delivery.

Deliveries will be made during regular business hours: Monday to Friday, from 10 am to 6 pm (excluding statutory holidays).

Our driver will contact you while en route to arrange a process for contactless delivery. Our driver can drop your bike off on your porch, driveway, or garage. For condo and apartment buildings, we ask that you meet the driver outside the main entrance of your building. Please have your order confirmation email and PIN number ready.

On the day of your scheduled delivery, our driver will contact you while en route to ensure delivery can be made, provide you with a window of time, and arrange a process for contactless delivery. Please have your order confirmation email and PIN number ready.

The driver will place your bike in the designated location, ring the doorbell, and move 10 feet away from the door. You will have the opportunity to inspect your bike to ensure it’s up to your standards. Once you are satisfied, the driver will ask for the confirmation PIN that has been provided in the order confirmation email and complete the delivery.

If the PIN does not match our driver’s records, the bike will be returned to the truck and we will investigate the order.

On the day of your scheduled delivery, our driver will contact you while en route to ensure delivery can be made. In the event that you are unable to receive your new bike, we will bring it back to our store for safe storage.

Curbside pick-up is not available at this time.

Questions About Your Order

At the time of delivery, you will be asked to inspect your bike and ensure it meets your expectations. If you are unhappy with your purchase, we will return the bike to our location at no extra charge and refund your purchase.

If you change your mind after a successful delivery has been made, please note that bikes cannot be shipped back to Sporting Life. We will, however, be happy to assist you with the return once our stores have reopened. Refunds will only be issued if the purchased bike is returned in new condition: clean, undamaged, with no wear on tires, grips or seats. Returns made in-store are not subject to a restocking fee.

Whether you change your mind during your bike inspection or return it to any of our stores when they reopen, you will not be charged a restocking fee.

Once our stores re-open, bring your bike into one of our bike shops. We will make any adjustments while you wait at no charge.

Only certified technicians can perform adjustments to a bike and cannot be done by our drivers. Unfortunately, we are unable to service bikes as all of our locations are closed until further notice.

All bike purchases are covered by our 3 Year Hassle-Free Warranty. Our team of technicians will be happy to make adjustments under this warranty when we re-open.

You can bring your bike to us for servicing once our stores have reopened.
