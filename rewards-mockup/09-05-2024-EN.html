Frequently Asked Questions

Simply create an account on our online store (www.sportinglife.ca) and say yes to sign up for Sporting Life rewards. When visiting our stores, please provide the cashier with your phone number or email address to continue earning rewards in-store.

We no longer mail coupons to home addresses. All coupons will be emailed to the primary email found on your rewards account. If you would like to merge your household account, please do so by logging into your account online, under your My Account > Rewards section you can select Associate Member. Just enter your associated members Member ID. A verification email will be sent to request the profile association, and a second verification email sent to you once it has been accepted, to confirm the association.

We no longer provide physical rewards cards. When shopping in-store please provide the cashier with your phone number or email address. When shopping online, please sign into your rewards account.

You can view your rewards in progress by logging into your account online. Select My Account > Rewards from the drop-down menu at the top of the page. This will bring you to your rewards page where all available rewards and rewards in progress are listed.

Simply log on to your online store profile, and under Merge Memberships, enter the membership that you would like to merge with the current member ID you are logged in under. A series of verification emails will be sent to the member's email account to ensure the merge is verified. Once done, all rewards will show up under one-member ID.

Coupon reactivation is analyzed on a case-by-case basis. We kindly ask that you contact the Customer Care department by using our Contact Us form to forward us your request.

The rewards points earned through the purchase of the returned item will be removed from the purchasing accounts cumulative rewards total and will be unable to be redeemed going forward.

Please ensure that the items in your cart match the conditions and details stipulated in the Reward notice e-mail. If you are still having issues applying a coupon, please contact the Customer Care department by using our Contact Us form and we can analyze the order contents for any redemption issues.

Please visit your Rewards Account page and click Edit Information under your customer profile. Once you have confirmed all the information is correct, you can press Apply. This should remedy any verification issues you may encounter. If you are still unable to see your rewards, please contact the Customer Care department by using our Contact Us form.

Rewards will not show in your accumulated total spend until your order has finished processing and been shipped to you. If your order is still not showing towards your spent total, please contact the Customer Care department by using our Contact Us form.

As long as you made the purchase with the same phone number used on your account, the points will be associated with your total automatically. If you require assistance in associating an order with your rewards account, please contact the Customer Care department by using our Contact Us form.
