Your ski boots provide control, steering, balance and comfort. With each of these, you'll be skiing as effectively as possible! Our in-store custom boot fitting services are perfect for skiers of any level, whether this is your first or fifth pair of boots.

1 Year Boot Fit Guarantee: If you are unhappy with your custom boot fit, please book an appointment to have your boots re-assessed within one year of the original fitting. Please note, fitting times may vary based on fitting requirements.

Custom boot fitting services may include:

  • Foot Assessment: Biomechanical review of the foot
  • Boot Modification: Shell punching, altering the liner, buckle adjustments
  • Boot Alignment: Cuff alignment, Potential canting of boot
