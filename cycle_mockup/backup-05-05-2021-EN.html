Bicycle Deliveries Delayed

Due to an unprecedented global shortage of bicycles and bicycle parts, our 2021 bicycle program is delayed. Bicycles will be arriving slowly throughout this spring and more frequently into the summer months. Please check our website regularly as it will be updated as new models arrive in stores. Bicycles marked “In Stock” are currently available for purchase. Bicycles will be available for purchase In-Store only.

Junior Bicycles estimated arrival April 15th – July 15th
Adult Bicycles estimated arrival May 15th – September 15th

Our Service Shops are OPEN

As a result of the global shortages, there is an increase in demand for servicing existing bicycles, which may lead to longer than normal wait times. Our service departments are open and committed to keeping you riding; however, we ask for your patience in these unprecedented times. To alleviate potential delays, we encourage customers to bring in their bikes as early as possible for service needs.
Please contact your local Sporting Life to book a service appointment prior to arrival. View our Services.
