What is the Retül Müve Dynamic Fit Bike?

The Retül Müve Dynamic Fit Bike is one of the world's most precise bike fitting tools. The Müve fit bike allows a fit technician to make dynamic, on the fly, micro adjustments to saddle, stem, bar, pedals and crank length, so you can experience fir differences immediately. Available at the Sporting Life 2665 Yonge St. location in Toronto, and at Sporting Life Lansdowne in Ottawa.
